LETTER FROM OUR RECRUITMENT CHAIR

Dear Potential New Members,

We in Phi Sigma Sigma are so excited to meet each of you as you participate in formal recruitment this January! We are eager to be a part of your journey in finding your home here in the University of Rochester Greek community and to introduce you to the sisterhood we share.

When I look back on my years here at U of R, I consider myself so lucky to have found the genuine sisterhood and endless support of Phi Sig. Since its beginning, our chapter has valued individuality above all else, defining ourselves not by any one stereotype, but rather letting our sisterhood be shaped by those within it. We are a network of friends who empower each other to strengthen our beliefs and gain the confidence to act on them. We are genuine, diverse, fun, unique, and open-minded; we are athletes, scholars, artists, leaders, and sisters. We are Phi Sigma Sigma.

I encourage you to make the first step and sign up for formal recruitment this spring and find an organization that you can call your home. If you have any questions about recruitment or our chapter, please do not hesitate to contact me!

Love In The Pyramid,


Emma Foley
efoley4@u.rochester.edu
Membership Recruitment Chair
Delta Beta Chapter
Phi Sigma Sigma
